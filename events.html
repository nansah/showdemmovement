The ShowDem
Jump Off

Represent your borough or city — come solo or with a partner! We'll be jumping, showing off moves, and having a full-on community celebration.

📅Date Saturday, July 18th, 2025
🕕Time 6:00 PM – 9:00 PM
📍Where Adam Clayton Powell Jr. State Office Building — 125th Street, Harlem, NYC
💰Cost Free to attend

Whether you're representing your borough in the battle, coming to jump and vibe with the community, or simply showing up to watch and socialize — the ShowDem Jump Off is for you. Come connect through Double Dutch, meet new people, and be part of the ShowDem energy.

Participants can register to jump solo or as a duo. All skill levels are welcome — from first-timers to seasoned double dutch veterans. The vibe is electric, the community is warm, and the ropes are ready.
