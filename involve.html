Join a Public Session

One-time check-in — cleared for all future sessions

ShowDem hosts regular community jump sessions open to all ages and skill levels. Complete our one-time check-in form and you're ready to participate in every future session — no re-registration ever.

  • Open to all ages and skill levels
  • One form, all future sessions
  • Community jump sessions, classes & events
  • Safe, joyful, and welcoming environment

Volunteer

Coach, mentor, organize, or cheer

ShowDem runs on community. Our volunteers are the backbone of every program, every event, and every session we deliver. Whether you're a double dutch expert or just someone with a big heart, there's a role for you.

  • Double Dutch Coach / Instructor
  • Youth Mentor & Role Model
  • Event Support & Setup
  • Admin, Communications & Outreach

  • Financial sponsorship or in-kind support
