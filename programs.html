Program 01

Free Community Sessions.

Throughout the year, Showdem hosts free public community sessions where people of all ages and experience levels can come together to learn, move, connect, and have fun through Double Dutch.

No experience required. No cost. Just come ready to move and be part of something special.

WhoAll ages, all backgrounds, all experience levels
CostFree & open to the public
WhereThroughout New York City
WhenYear-round — follow us for announcements
