The Heartbeat

Double Dutch Is More Than a Game

Two ropes turning in sync. Feet flying. A crowd counting along. Double dutch is rhythm, trust, and community in motion — and it's been bringing people together for generations.

At ShowDem, we honor that legacy while making it new — creating space for every skill level, every age, and every community.

Our Story

Built on Love, Powered by Community

ShowDem Movement Foundation was born from a simple belief: every child deserves to be seen, celebrated, and supported.

Founded by Martha Igbinosa, ShowDem channels the energy of double dutch into programs that uplift young people and strengthen the communities around them.

We believe talent is everywhere. What it needs is a stage, a coach, and a crowd that believes in it.
