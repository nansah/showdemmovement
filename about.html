How It Started

A Movement Born from Purpose

ShowDem Movement Foundation was born from a simple, powerful belief: every child deserves to be seen, celebrated, and supported. In a world that often overlooks youth — especially those in underserved communities — ShowDem exists to change that, one rope turn at a time.

Founded by Martha Igbinosa, ShowDem takes the energy, rhythm, and community spirit of double dutch and channels it into something larger: a movement that educates, uplifts, and empowers.

"We don't just jump ropes. We jump over barriers — and we do it together."

From community jump sessions in neighborhood parks to organized showcases where youth perform in front of crowds, ShowDem is building a world where talent gets a stage, effort gets recognized, and every young person knows they belong.

What makes ShowDem different isn't just the ropes. It's the relationships. The mentors who show up. The coaches who believe. The community that cheers — not just for the winners, but for everyone who has the courage to step in.

Meet the Founder

Martha Igbinosa

Founder & Executive Director

Martha Igbinosa is the heart and engine of ShowDem Movement Foundation. A community builder, educator, and double dutch advocate, Martha launched ShowDem with a vision to create spaces where youth are celebrated, supported, and inspired to lead.

Her background in community organizing, youth development, and cultural programming gives ShowDem its distinctive approach: grounded in love, driven by purpose, and always rooted in the community it serves.

Martha believes that talent is everywhere — and that what communities need isn't charity, but opportunity, visibility, and someone who believes in them. ShowDem is that someone.

"Love in our hearts. Purpose in our actions. Impact in our communities."
